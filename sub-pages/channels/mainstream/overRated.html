Instagram Stories

Paula Glab

Instagram has recently unveiled a new feature with it’s update, yet at the same time this feature seems very familiar, mostly because they obviously bootlegged it from snapchat. Instagram, most likely in it’s thirst to bring people back into the app, has added on a story feature, that allows a user to basically do the same thing that snapchat does with it’s story feature.

What annoys me most is the fact that some people are actually using it, specifically older people who think this is “cool” and “original”. Yet, many people in Gen Z specifically know that this is in fact a fraud. Instagram is a power hungry company that obviously needs to stop this. It’s making me cringe just thinking about it. Honestly, most people are using this feature to promote their snapchats. That’s just how ironic all of this is. Instagram, check yourself.
