The recent popularity of Pokemon GO has been one of the most talked about things both in the United States and outside as well. If you take a random person’s phone on the street,there is a guarantee that they will be playing Pokemon GO. Yet, one of the things that most people weren’t expecting from Pokemon GO was the new support that local economies across the country are getting from people playing Pokemon GO.

For example, if you’re forced to walk around outside for hours on end trying to hatch that one egg, you’re gonna need some food and drinks. So, your instincts tell you to go to your local deli, or convenience store. Having all these people forced to walk around their neighborhoods to catch pokemon has allowed local businesses the exposure they need in order to attract more customers.

Pokemon GO overall has been a revolutionary game; both for players and for the company. But, in the end, another winner in all this is local businesses, who are now able to keep thriving in a world of corporate business through a game that forces people to walk around and do stuff.
