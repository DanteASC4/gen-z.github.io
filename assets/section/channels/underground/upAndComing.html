Up and coming music

author: Joey Cabrera

Grime music is a pretty underground genre, when I first heard it I thought it was basically electro rap from the UK. Grime is a genre of music that emerged in East London in the early 2000s. It is primarily a development of UK garage and jungle, Prominent artists that stylized this genre include Wiley, Dizzee Rascal, Ghetts, Kano, Lethal Bizzle, and Skepta.Prominent Grime crews include: Boy Better Know, Newham Generals, Roll Deep, Ruff Sqwad.

Just like rock has different types of rock such as alternative rock, screamo, heavy metal, country rock, rap has now officially become as diverse as far as to have many different types of rap, we now have old school, trap, horrorcore, and now Grime is emerging as a whole new type of rap. Grime is still underground but definitely emerging.

In the UK, Grime is extremely big alongside with UK trap or dubstep. Everywhere else around the world it is not that big. Despite its popularity growing in europe and america. Who knows what music has in store for us in the future and what grime will bring to the scene.
