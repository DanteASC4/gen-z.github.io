The most underrated

Author: Joey Cabrera

Rap

The most underrated rappers in the scene right now are Earl Sweatshirt, Joey Badass, Vince Staples, Keith ape, and Rich chigga . All of these rappers bring something a little bit different to the rap game The rise in rap in asia has created rappers like Keith ape and Rich chigga. Rich chigga originally from indonesia has created a huge wave with rap in china and Keith ape has created a huge wave with rap in Korea and Japan. Earl Sweatshirt brings a more melancholy vibe to the rap game, Joey Badass updates the rap game on how New York is doing and Vince Staples updates the rap game on the slums of California.

bands

The most underrated bands are the wavves, the xx, and the gorillaz . They all have very unique vibes and mellows which makes these bands so special. Wavves connects with the lo-fi surf rock skate scene, it pretty underground but it is on the rise and people should definitely listen to them. The xx has a vibe of its own, if I could I would give it it’s own category in music. I say that the xx has a special, lovely relaxed vibe that makes you think of your loved ones for the good and the bad.The gorillaz are making a comeback as they’ve fallen from the face of the earth, as of now, many believe they are irrelevant but they are dropping a new album soon and I believe it will show that the gorillaz is forever.
